# VISION.md

The digital nervous system for Detroit techno and its surrounding ecosystem. Combining the event-aggregator power of RA (Resident Advisor), the marketplace utility of Bandcamp/Discogs, the local business community-building of the Metro Times, and modern social feeds.

By pulling in data from Movement Parties, Tectroit, and RA, while giving local brick-and-mortars a direct pipeline to electronic music fans, we create an ecosystem where subculture directly supports local commerce.

Core foundational concepts:
- Dark mode industrial minimalist aesthetic by default (Techno flyers inspired).
- Heavy accentuation on 4 core profile models: User, Artist (DJs), Business, and Non-Profit.
- Deliver absolute type safety and robust error handling for scraping and marketplace flows.
